Some simple physics/facts you should know to really appreciate the subject!

  • Astronomical unit (distance from earth to sun): 1 A.U. = 150×106 km
  • Temperature of the photosphere of the sun: 6000 K
  • If an object moves a distance d in a time t traveling at a velocity v, then : d = v t
  • The wavelength λ, the frequency ν and the speed c of electromagnetic ratiation (or any other kind of wave) are related by : λ ν = c
  • The energy radiated by a blackbody per unit surface area per unit time is related to the temperature of the surface by : I = σ T4
  • The wavelength of peak intensity of blackbody radiation is related to the temperature by : λmax = constant/T
  • The energy of a single photon is related to its frequency by : E = hν - where h is a constant (“Planck’s constant”)
